Goals:

The Software should provide a platform which hosts the solutions to problems that have been solved in conversations over phone in the past.

Goal 1: As an always accessible website it should help parents with:
Checking times for courses and tournaments
Checking needed equipments
Checking where a event takes place
getting telephone numbers and other ways to contact trainers

Goal 2: Trainers need assistence with:
providing infos about courses such as times, equipment, places and contact
adding and removing events

Goal 3: E-Mail notifications
Members should be able to allow E-Mail notifications that inform them about changes in the events.

Sophie "I want to check the training times and places of my children without calling the trainer." Sophie is 35 years old and is a mother of two. Her children are 10 and 12 years old. She has only basic computer skills like opening a webpage. Uses her smartphone and her laptop to access the internet - stressed from work, likes simplicity in her daily routine - wants good service for her money - has everything planned and needs easy access to training times of her kids - Lose stress after work because of managing her kids' hobbies by having a platform with infos. "Two times a week I come home after work ready to relax a bit before bringing my kids to their training. One of my kids is getting ready to go to her roller skating training but she doesn't know which equipment she needs. I now have to call her trainer (whos number I have saved somewhere in my paperwork) and ask him about the equipment. He doesn't pick up until the third try. All in all this takes me half an hour which means there is no time to relax before leaving home again."
Michael "I need to manage times and equipment online for my students so they don't have to call me everytime" Michael is 28 years old and works as a trainer in a roller skating club while studying at a university. He has standard computer skills and knows how to handle online accounts and basic permissions. He uses his smartphone and a laptop to access the internet - before work he has to study and doesn't want to get calls all the time - only gets payed for his training hours - has much info that changes quickly and no easy way to communicate changes with parents - hard cut between work, studies and life without dayly phone calls "Everyday after class I look at my phone and I have several missed calls by furious parents who need infos about the training. It's stressful and hard to manage all that while telling every parent the same stuff instead of having a board for every parent to read. Even after all that there are kids who show up at canceled classes because they couldn't get the info."
